Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where is the event being held?
    The even is being held at Clemson University's Watt Family Innovation Center. The address is 405 S Palmetto Blvd, Clemson, SC 29634.
  • When is the event?
    CUCTF is taking place October 3rd, 2020.
  • COVID-19?
    error_outline

    CU Cyber is aware of the lingering uncertainty with the COVID-19 pandemic. Our hearts are with those affected by the virus, and our leadership team is taking the steps necessary to ensure CUCTF 2020 is up and operational come October 3rd.

    At this time, Clemson University has not released a statement regarding whether or not Fall Semester will operate under normal conditions. If Clemson University does not permit this event to take place, CUCTF 2020 will become a virtual event that will allow universities to participate in the event remotely. We will implement this plan by using the following strategy:

    • Present opening and closing ceremonies via Zoom
    • Utilize Slack/Discord to maintain a connection with participants
    • Increase CTF infrastructure to allow more universities to participate online
  • Who is eligible for the event?
    Only university and college teams are eligible for CUCTF.
  • How many people are allowed on a team?
    You may have up to 4 members on a competing team and, optionally, up to 2 members for the CTF Bootcamp.
  • Do we get anything for winning?
    Yes! We'll announce the prizes and update this FAQ once we have more information on awards.
  • What is the CTF bootcamp's purpose?
    The CTF bootcamp serves to allow participating schools to bring additional members to CUCTF 2020. These additional members will solve basic CTF problems guided by CU Cyber mentors and have the opportunity to have a "CTF experience" regardless of their background in cyber security!
