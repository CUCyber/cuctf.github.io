CUCTF 2019

CUCTF 2019 was our first time hosting a capture the flag event, and it was a blast! From the array of sponsors, excited universities, and helpful staff, the atmosphere was electric. We can't wait for CUCTF 2020!

The event included:

  • 8 Reverse Engineering challenges
  • 9 Binary Exploitation challenges
  • 7 Web Exploitation challenges
  • 9 Cryptography challenges
  • 6 Forensics challenges
  • 10 Trivia challenges
