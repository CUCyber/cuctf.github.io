Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the event being held?

The event is being held at Clemson University's Watt Center. The address is 405 S Palmetto Blvd, Clemson, SC 29634.


When is the event?

CUCTF is taking place September 28th, 2019.


Who is elgible for the event?

Only universities are eligible for CUCTF.


How many people are allowed on a team?

To participate in CUCTF, you must have one faculty advisor, up to four players in the CTF, and up to two players in the CTF bootcamp.


Do we get anything for winning?

First Place Team: $1k
Second Place Team: $500
Third Place Team: $250


Do we need a faculty advisor?

No, a faculty advisor is not necessary to participate in CUCTF.
